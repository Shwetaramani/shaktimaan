AcePoints - A unique player reward program of Shaktimaan games.
Get rewarded with AcePoints every time you play cash games and premium entry tournaments. The more you play, the more AcePoints you earn.

Earn AcePoints

Earn 1 AcePoint for every 100 Rupees you play on Shaktimaan cash games and premium entry tournaments.

As you earn AcePoints, you will reach to higher Ace Levels. The number of stars corresponding to each Ace Level will appear alongside your Username.

Ace Levels and Level Upgrades

As you earn AcePoints, your Reward Level gets automatically upgraded to the next level based on the table below.

Benefits of AceLevels

There are several benefits of Ace Levels and the higher your Ace Level the higher benefits you get during certain cash addition bonus promotions, ranging from higher bonus % to higher max bonus amount.

In Addition some other benefits are listed below:

  • Gold Acelevel Player will be able to do a cash addition of ₹30,000 in a single transaction
  • Platinum & Platinum+ AceLevel Players can do a cash addition of ₹30,000 in a single transaction
  • Gold & Platinum AceLevel Players can redeem upto ₹20,000 in a single transaction
  • Platinum+ AceLevel Players can redeem upto ₹30,000 in a single transaction
